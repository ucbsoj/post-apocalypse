Are We Living in the Post-Apocalypse?

Maybe the World Has Already Ended

Later this summer, everyone is going to be talking about the “Golden Spike”, a momentous geological stage marking the beginning of the period on Earth called the Anthropocene. The summer of 2023 is the time to ask ourselves what it truly means to be living during the time known as Anthropocene.

How do we define the world, and how do we move on? Answers to these questions, or perhaps simply better questions altogether, emerge from the story of a UC Berkeley Human Geographer, his friendship with a Botanist, and a photographer dedicated to visually defining the times we are living in.
